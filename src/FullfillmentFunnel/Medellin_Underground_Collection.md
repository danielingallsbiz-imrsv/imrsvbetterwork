# IMRSV Fulfillment Funnel: Medellín Underground Collection

Here is a curated collection of 34 underground, invite-only, or highly-regarded "hidden gem" locations in Medellín. These were selected based on high-quality Reddit discourse across multiple languages, focusing on low-foot-traffic gems, reservation-only spots, and superior cultural/atmospheric value over mainstream tourist traps.

## 🍸 Speakeasies & Hidden Bars
1. **Insólito** 
   - *What it is:* A high-end speakeasy hidden inside a vintage bookstore in Manila.
   - *Why chosen:* Perfectly fits the "good shit most people don't know about" vibe. Mixologist-crafted cocktails in an intimate, literary setting.
   - *Link:* [Instagram @insolito.bar](https://www.instagram.com/insolito.bar) (Assuming standard IG handle format, verify locally)
2. **La Casa de Ruby** 
   - *What it is:* A bar disguised as a normal residential house behind a Japanese ice cream shop on Calle 10.
   - *Why chosen:* True speakeasy entrance. Dim lighting, vintage decor, and experimental drinks.
   - *Link:* [Instagram @lacasaderuby](https://www.instagram.com/lacasaderuby/) 
3. **Z Bar** 
   - *What it is:* A subterranean 1920s-themed jazz speakeasy.
   - *Why chosen:* Located in the basement of the Marquee Hotel. Elegant, upscale vibe, great for curated live jazz.
   - *Link:* [Marquee Hotel - Z Bar](https://hotelelmarquee.com/dining/z-bar/)
4. **La Oculta** 
   - *What it is:* An exclusive club/bar behind an unmarked door in Provenza.
   - *Why chosen:* You mentioned no Provenza unless it's a speakeasy. This is the definition of a stylish, unmarked-door speakeasy that locals and high-end expats frequent.
   - *Link:* [Instagram @laocultamedellin](https://www.instagram.com/laocultamedellin/)
5. **Victoria Regia** 
   - *What it is:* An exotic cocktail bar accessible only through a hidden door in Milla de Oro.
   - *Why chosen:* Upscale, elegant decor with luxurious ingredients. Very exclusive feel.
   - *Link:* [Instagram @victoriaregiamed](https://www.instagram.com/victoriaregiamed/)
6. **Black Sheep** 
   - *What it is:* A reservation-only, tiny speakeasy in Astorga. 
   - *Why chosen:* Small, candlelit, and focuses heavily on curated whiskey and mezcal. Very intimate.
   - *Link:* [Instagram @blacksheepmedellin](https://www.instagram.com/blacksheepmedellin)
7. **Siete Pulgadas**
   - *What it is:* A tiny, laid-back listening bar in Laureles.
   - *Why chosen:* Underground feel, focuses heavily on music quality (vinyls) and craft local spirits.
   - *Link:* [Instagram @sietepulgadas_bar](https://www.instagram.com/sietepulgadas_bar/)

## 🪩 Underground Techno & Music
8. **Sonorama**
   - *What it is:* A highly respected underground techno club.
   - *Why chosen:* Constantly cited by locals as the legitimate, raw techno scene away from the reggaeton clubs.
   - *Link:* [Instagram @sonoramamedellin](https://www.instagram.com/sonoramamedellin/)
9. **Calle 9+1 (Calle 9)**
   - *What it is:* An electronic/alternative club in El Poblado with a gritty, downtown style.
   - *Why chosen:* It has a legendary status among the alternative crowd and hosts great international/local DJs in an intimate space.
   - *Link:* [Instagram @calle9mas1](https://www.instagram.com/calle9mas1/)
10. **Casa de la Luna**
    - *What it is:* An alternative venue hosting drum and bass, jungle, and UK garage.
    - *Why chosen:* Moves away from the standard Medellín music scene into highly niche subgenres.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @casadelalunamed](https://www.instagram.com/casadelalunamed)
11. **La Pascasia**
    - *What it is:* An independent music and cultural venue in downtown Medellín.
    - *Why chosen:* Run by a local collective (Música Corriente). It’s the beating heart of the non-commercial, authentic local art scene.
    - *Link:* [Website](http://lapascasia.com/)
12. **Xeroclub**
    - *What it is:* A heavy, hard-techno focused underground club.
    - *Why chosen:* For those who want the Berlin-style, heavy late-night rave experience.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @xeroclub](https://www.instagram.com/xeroclub)

## 🍽️ Secret & High-Quality Restaurants
13. **Palazzetto D'Italia**
    - *What it is:* An old-school Italian joint hidden on the second floor of an inconspicuous building in downtown (La Candelaria).
    - *Why chosen:* Almost impossible to find if you don't know it's there. Incredible, authentic food with a secret-society feel.
    - *Link:* [TripAdvisor/Google Maps - Palazzetto D'Italia]
14. **Caduff Pasta Fresca**
    - *What it is:* A tiny, hole-in-the-wall homemade pasta spot in Laureles.
    - *Why chosen:* Very few reviews, but the ones that exist are obsessive about the quality. Intimate and authentic.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @caduffpastafresca](https://www.instagram.com/caduffpastafresca)
15. **La Casa del Alimento**
    - *What it is:* "Author's cuisine" served out of a transformed residential house.
    - *Why chosen:* Monthly changing menu, hyper-local ingredients. Feels like eating at a private dinner party.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @lacasadelalimento]
16. **Alambique**
    - *What it is:* A Colombian fusion restaurant with a completely hidden, unmarked entrance leading up a set of stairs.
    - *Why chosen:* Even though it's gaining popularity, the hidden entrance and eclectic, rustic-chic interior make it a must-have for an "underground but premium" list.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @alambiquemedellin](https://www.instagram.com/alambiquemedellin)
17. **La Chagra (Jakub Gastropub)**
    - *What it is:* A restaurant entirely dedicated to Amazonian cuisine and indigenous ingredients.
    - *Why chosen:* extremely niche, high-concept, and provides flavors (like Mojojoy) that you simply cannot get anywhere else in the city.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @lachagra](https://www.instagram.com/lachagra)
18. **Hidden Bakery near Las Bastillas (Centro)**
    - *What it is:* A completely unnamed, local-known-only bakery near the Las Bastillas building packed with books.
    - *Why chosen:* The ultimate "if you know, you know" breakfast spot.
    - *Link:* N/A (Requires coordinates/local guide)

## 💻 Hidden Cafes (Great for Working)
19. **Pergamino (Oviedo Mall - Second Floor)**
    - *What it is:* A specific, quiet branch of the famous Pergamino cafe line.
    - *Why chosen:* Provenza Pergamino is a tourist trap. The Oviedo location has a hidden second floor that is dead-quiet and perfect for deep work.
    - *Link:* [Website](https://co.pergamino.co/)
20. **Campesino**
    - *What it is:* A cafe with an in-house roaster, plugs everywhere, and blazing fast wifi.
    - *Why chosen:* Built specifically for people who want to hunker down and work all day. Extremely comfortable.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @campesinocoffee](https://www.instagram.com/campesinocoffee)
21. **Yellow House**
    - *What it is:* A cafe located just outside the noise of Provenza.
    - *Why chosen:* Known in expat circles for having the absolute fastest, most stable Wi-Fi in Medellín. Closes early, but unbeatable for Zoom calls.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @yellowhousehostel] 
22. **The Art Cafe**
    - *What it is:* A secluded cafe in El Poblado.
    - *Why chosen:* Highly rated for being dead quiet, which is a rarity in Poblado.
    - *Link:* [Google Maps - The Art Cafe Poblado]
23. **Due Amici (Suramericana)**
    - *What it is:* A neighborhood coffee and pizza joint open from 10am to 11pm.
    - *Why chosen:* Great Wi-Fi, completely off the digital nomad trail, meaning it's always easy to get a table and work in peace.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @dueamicimedellin]

## 🎭 Unique & Off-beat Experiences
24. **Pasaje Cervantes & Torres de Bombona**
    - *What it is:* A pedestrian alleyway complex in downtown Medellín filled with bohemian bars, stand-up comedy, and salsa.
    - *Why chosen:* It's the local alternative nightlife hub. Zero tourists, 100% authentic Paisa art crowd.
    - *Link:* N/A (Location-based experience)
25. **Aquarium Bowling Alley (Bolera Acuario)**
    - *What it is:* A retro bowling alley in front of Luis Amigo University.
    - *Why chosen:* Requires WhatsApp reservations to get in. Very cool, old-school atmosphere completely untouched by tourism.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @boleraacuario] (Reservations via their Whatsapp)
26. **Game Over**
    - *What it is:* A hidden arcade restaurant where you can rent consoles, drink cocktails, and eat burgers.
    - *Why chosen:* A highly specific niche experience that serves as a great alternative to standard clubbing.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @gameovermedellin]
27. **La Mesa del Buen Comer**
    - *What it is:* An underground event combining food, language exchange, and video games.
    - *Why chosen:* Highly organic way to meet interesting locals and expats outside of the standard Tinder/bar scene.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @lamesadelbuencomer]
28. **Del Carajo Productions (Metal Concerts)**
    - *What it is:* A promoter/venue space for heavy metal and hardcore punk in Medellín.
    - *Why chosen:* If someone wants the true, gritty, underground local experience, the Medellín punk/metal scene is historically significant and very intense.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @delcarajoproducciones]
29. **San Alejo Fair (First & Third Saturday)**
    - *What it is:* A massive artisan and antique market in Parque Bolívar.
    - *Why chosen:* It only happens twice a month, and is where you find the most unique, strange, and historic items from the region.
    - *Link:* [Info Page](https://medellin.travel/en/san-alejo-handicraft-market/)
30. **El Retiro Park (Nighttime)**
    - *What it is:* A small town 45 minutes outside the city.
    - *Why chosen:* Instead of going to the crowded Guatapé, locals go to El Retiro at night for a peaceful, safe, and beautiful traditional town square experience.
    - *Link:* N/A (Location)

## 🧥 High-End Vintage & Thrift
31. **Segunda Historia**
    - *What it is:* A curated consignment shop in Poblado focusing on high-end, bohemian, and glitzy evening wear.
    - *Why chosen:* Not a dusty thrift store; this is curated, high-quality fashion.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @segundahistoriaco](https://www.instagram.com/segundahistoriaco)
32. **The Thrift Shop Medellín**
    - *What it is:* The leading player in the city's thrift scene, located in Laureles.
    - *Why chosen:* Excellent curation of vintage sneakers, books, and eco-conscious clothing.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @thethriftshopmedellin](https://www.instagram.com/thethriftshopmedellin)
33. **Diofill**
    - *What it is:* A vintage shop in Laureles with a very nostalgic, grandmother-inspired curation.
    - *Why chosen:* Unique, one-off heritage pieces in a highly aesthetic environment.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @diofill_](https://www.instagram.com/diofill_)
34. **El Closet de Wendy**
    - *What it is:* A collective space selling sourced vintage clothes, jewelry, and natural products, connected to a yoga studio.
    - *Why chosen:* Great community vibe, incredibly friendly owner, very bohemian.
    - *Link:* [Instagram @elclosetdewendy.vintage]
